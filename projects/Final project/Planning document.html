Bountiful foods

Website Planning Document

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to increase earnings for Bountiful foods, especially through delivery. As the site acts as both advertising, and a store front to online users. As well as a place that customers can try out and buy different healthy and flavorful fruit combinations.

Target Audience

We are looking for people who are aged 25 or older, mainly due to the fact that they would have more money than younger individuals. Probably do the shopping for the family, and need to worry more about their health.

Gender is more Femail than male, but probably not by a large margin. As women tend to do the shopping more than men.

We are targeting those with a higher education level.

We are targeting those with a higher income, as they will be more likely to spend their money. And poor people often aren`t as interested with health, as they are more concerned with baseline survival.

We will likely have other farmers interested in this cite, but its debatable as to whether or not we want to cater to them. As they are the competition.

Scenarios

  • How will I feed my family this week?
  • Where can I find fresh produce grown locally?
  • How should I start my new diet?
  • Where can I sell my produce?
  • What should I have for supper?
