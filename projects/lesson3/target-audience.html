Target Audience

The target audience is generally people who are looking to be more involved in the lethrbidge community. This demographic is pretty much exclusively adults, and would mostly be those of higher income with time to spare. The most likely way they are acessing the site is through computer, though I would like it to be able to work just as well on a phone.
